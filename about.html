About the Project

"Institutional Market Intelligence for Independent Investors"

US Market Terminals is a specialized financial research platform. We leverage advanced data visualization and quantitative modeling to democratize institutional-grade market insights for the global investing community.

Our Core Vision

In the complex financial landscape of 2026, information asymmetry remains the biggest hurdle for individual investors. Our vision is to empower every independent trader with the analytical tools, real-time data terminals, and systematic research necessary to make anti-fragile, data-driven decisions in US equities and digital asset markets.

Our Specialized Services

Nasdaq Growth Analytics Quantitative breakdown of Nasdaq-100 constituents, focusing on AI-driven productivity and FCF yield.
Dividend Cash Flow Engine Strategic tracking of Dividend Aristocrats and REITs using our proprietary AFFO sustainability models.
Crypto Matrix Terminal Comprehensive on-chain data analysis and staking yield monitoring for institutional-grade digital assets.
Macro Research Reports Deep-dive strategic insights on Federal Reserve policies, inflation hedges, and sector rotation.

Analytical Values

1. Data Transparency: Every report published is grounded in verifiable public filings (SEC) and real-time API data streams.

2. Objective Neutrality: We eliminate emotional bias by focusing strictly on mathematical indicators and historical backtesting.

3. Long-Term Sustainability: Our research prioritizes wealth preservation and compounding growth over short-term noise.
