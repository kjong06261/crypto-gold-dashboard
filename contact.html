About US Market Terminals

US Market Terminals is an independent educational platform providing market data visualization tools and educational content about financial markets. Our tools display publicly available market data through embedded widgets from data providers like TradingView.

We focus on four main areas: U.S. equity markets (with emphasis on the Nasdaq 100), dividend-paying securities, cryptocurrency markets, and precious metals. Our goal is to present market information in an accessible format while providing educational context to help visitors understand what they're seeing.

This website does not provide investment advice, recommendations, or personalized financial guidance. All content is for educational and informational purposes only. We encourage visitors to conduct their own research and consult qualified professionals before making any financial decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is US Market Terminals free to use?

Yes. All tools and content on this website are available at no cost. We may display advertisements to support the operation of the site.

Where does your market data come from?

Market data is displayed through embedded widgets from third-party providers such as TradingView. We do not independently verify the accuracy of this data. For official financial data, please refer to primary sources such as exchange websites or regulatory filings.

Can you recommend stocks or tell me what to buy?

No. US Market Terminals provides educational content only. We do not offer investment advice, recommendations, or personalized financial guidance. Please consult a qualified financial advisor for investment decisions.

How often is the content updated?

Market data widgets update in real-time or with a slight delay depending on the data provider. Educational articles are updated periodically as needed. Check publication dates on individual articles for currency.

I found an error on your site. How do I report it?

Please use the contact form above to let us know about any errors, broken links, or outdated information. We appreciate your help in keeping the site accurate.
