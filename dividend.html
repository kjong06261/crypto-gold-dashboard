1. Strategic Income: The Power of Compound Dividends

Dividend investing is more than just collecting a check; it's about the Yield on Cost and long-term capital preservation. Our dashboard tracks key metrics such as Payout Ratios, Dividend Growth Rates (5-year CAGR), and the reliability of monthly payers like Realty Income (O). Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) provide a unique gateway to professional-grade property management income. By analyzing AFFO (Adjusted Funds From Operations), we help users distinguish between a sustainable 6% yield and a dangerous "yield trap." We focus on triple-net lease structures and data center REITs that are poised to benefit from the ongoing AI infrastructure boom in 2026.
