2. Understanding the Chowder Rule in 2026

Strategic dividend investing is not about the current yield alone; it is about Dividend Growth. Institutional investors often use the Chowder Rule: (Current Yield + 5-Year Dividend Growth Rate). For 2026, we look for a Chowder number above 8% for defensive names. This ensures that your purchasing power is not just maintaining pace with inflation, but expanding wealth over time.
