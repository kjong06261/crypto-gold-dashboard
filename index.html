06 · About

An independent research bureau.

Dividend Forensics Bureau (DFB) analyzes U.S. income assets through structural frameworks — coverage, buffer, maturity — rather than headlines.

Founded by Jeong-Mo Goo, DFB publishes across writing channels (Substack, Benzinga, Medium, GuruFocus) and visual channels (Instagram, Threads, Pinterest), with the Shieldy mascot serving as the visual seal of the framework system.

DFB does not sell time. DFB sells the framework.
