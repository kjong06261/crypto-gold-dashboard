Why US Market Terminals?

The modern financial market is flooded with data. Successful investing comes not from gathering information, but from filtering noise and identifying meaningful signals. US Market Terminals is a web-based platform designed to equip individual investors with institutional-grade intelligence.

Core Philosophy: The Barbell Strategy

We advocate the 'Barbell Strategy'. One end of the portfolio focuses on capital appreciation through Nasdaq-100 innovation leaders, while the other secures stable Cash Flow via REITs and Dividend Aristocrats to hedge against market volatility. Additionally, Digital Assets (Crypto) are incorporated as satellite holdings to capture alpha returns.

Data-Driven Decisions

All investment decisions should be based on data. We utilize key metrics such as:

  • FCF Yield (Free Cash Flow): Evaluating a company's true cash-generating ability.
  • FFO/AFFO (Funds From Operations): The gold standard for analyzing REIT health.
  • Dividend Growth Rate: The power of dividend stocks to hedge against inflation.
  • On-chain Metrics: Analyzing Bitcoin network activity and holder distribution.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the difference between Nasdaq-100 and S&P 500?

The Nasdaq-100 is a growth-oriented index heavily weighted towards technology and biotech companies. The S&P 500 represents the broader market with 500 large-cap companies across diverse sectors, offering relatively more stability.

What is the most important metric for Dividend Stocks?

The Payout Ratio and Dividend Growth Rate are critical. Even with a high yield, a payout ratio over 100% indicates unsustainability. Look for a history of at least 5 years of consecutive dividend growth.

What is the best strategy for Bitcoin investing?

Due to high volatility, DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) is highly recommended. It is also essential to monitor on-chain metrics and macroeconomic indicators like interest rates and the Dollar Index to manage risk.
